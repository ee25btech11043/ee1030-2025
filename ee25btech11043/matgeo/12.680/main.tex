\textbf{Question}:\
The rank of matrix is
\begin{align*}
\myvec{
1 & 2 & 2 & 3\\
3 & 4 & 2 & 5\\
5 & 6 & 2 & 7\\
7 & 8 & 2 & 9
}
\end{align*}

\textbf{Solution: }
Let the given matrix be $\vec{A}$:
\begin{align}
\vec{A}=\myvec{
1 & 2 & 2 & 3\\
3 & 4 & 2 & 5\\
5 & 6 & 2 & 7\\
7 & 8 & 2 & 9
}
\end{align}


    $R_2 \rightarrow R_2-3R_1$ , $R_3 \rightarrow R_3-5R_1$ , $R_4 \rightarrow R_4-7R_1$

    \begin{align}
    \myvec{
    1 & 2 & 2 & 3\\
    0 & -2 & -4 & -4\\
    0 & -4 & -8 & -8\\
    0 & -6 & -12 & -12
    }
    \end{align}

    $R_2 &\rightarrow -\frac{1}{2}R_2$ 
    \begin{align}
    \myvec{
    1 & 2 & 2 & 3\\
    0 & 1 & 2 & 2\\
    0 & -4 & -8 & -8\\
    0 & -6 & -12 & -12
    }
    \end{align}

    $R_3 \rightarrow R_3+4R_2$ , $R_4 \rightarrow R_4+6R_2$ , $R_1 \rightarrow R_1-2R_2$
    \begin{align}
    \myvec{
    1 & 0 & -2 & -1\\
    0 & 1 & 2 & 2\\
    0 & 0 & 0 & 0\\
    0 & 0 & 0 & 0
    }
    \end{align}

The number of non-zero rows (pivot rows) in the row-echelon form is 2.\\
Therefore, the rank of the matrix $A$ is 2.
