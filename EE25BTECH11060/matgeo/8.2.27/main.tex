\textbf{Question}\\Find Equation of curve whose Focus is $\brak{0,-3}$ and Directrix y=3\\
\textbf{Solution}\\
General Equation of a conic is given by\\
\begin{align}
\Vec{x}^\top \Vec{V} \Vec{x} +2 \Vec{u}^\top \Vec{x}+f=0
\end{align}
where
\begin{align}
\Vec{V}=||\Vec{n}||^2 \Vec{I} -e^2  \Vec{n} \Vec{n} ^\top \\
\Vec{u}=ce^2\Vec{n}-||\Vec{n}||^2 \Vec{F}\\
f=||\Vec{n}||^2 ||\Vec{F}||^2 -c^2 e^2
\end{align}
Given,
\begin{align*}
    e=1  \quad 
    \Vec{F}=\begin{pmatrix}
        0 \\ -3
    \end{pmatrix}\\
    \Vec{n}=\begin{pmatrix}
        0 \\ 1
    \end{pmatrix}  \quad 
    c = 3
\end{align*}
From given equations,
\begin{align}
    \Vec{V}=\begin{pmatrix}
        1 & 0  \\
        0 & 1
    \end{pmatrix}-\begin{pmatrix}
        1 & 0
    \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
        1 \\  0
    \end{pmatrix}\\
   \Vec{V} =\begin{pmatrix}
        1  & 0 \\ 0 & 0
    \end{pmatrix}\\
    \Vec{u}=3 \begin{pmatrix}
        0 \\ 1
    \end{pmatrix}-\begin{pmatrix}
        0  \\ -3
    \end{pmatrix}\\
   \Vec{u} =\begin{pmatrix}
        0 \\ 6
    \end{pmatrix}\\
    f=9-9 =0 
\end{align}
From equation 1,
\begin{align}
    \Vec{x}^\top \begin{pmatrix}
        1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0
    \end{pmatrix} \Vec{x} + 2 \begin{pmatrix}
        0 \\ 6
    \end{pmatrix} ^ \top \Vec{x} =0 
\end{align}
